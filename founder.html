hadar cohen

hadar is a feminist seeking to open up gender inequality conversation in the tech world. she studied electrical engineering at cooper union and worked at various startups. she has experienced the impact of lack of female leadership and a male dominated environment in her studies.

disheartened by the pervasive culture of gender discrimination, hadar left the tech world to work on an urban farm. connecting to land and cultivating produce inspired her to think about our culture in more creative and profound ways.

hadar is excited by worlds colliding. merging the farm world with tech culture is an illuminating and exciting endeavor. she firmly believes that tech will gain an immeasurable amount by investing in diversity initiatives.
